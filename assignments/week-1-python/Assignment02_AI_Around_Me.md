# Assignment 02 – AI Around Me

## Problem Statement
List 10 real-life AI applications used daily and explain in 1–2 lines how Machine Learning works behind each.

---

## Solution

1. **Google Search**  
Uses Machine Learning to understand search intent and rank pages based on relevance, previous searches, and user behaviour.

2. **YouTube Recommendations**  
ML analyzes watch history, likes, and watch time to recommend videos using recommendation algorithms.

3. **Instagram Feed & Reels**  
AI predicts content users will engage with most by analyzing likes, comments, shares, and viewing time.

4. **Amazon Product Suggestions**  
Uses collaborative filtering to recommend products based on similar user behaviour and purchase history.

5. **Netflix Recommendations**  
ML models analyze viewing patterns and ratings to suggest personalized movies and series.

6. **Google Maps Traffic Prediction**  
AI processes real-time location data to predict traffic and suggest faster routes.

7. **Gmail Spam Filter**  
Machine Learning models classify emails as spam or important using trained datasets.

8. **Spotify Music Recommendations**  
ML analyzes listening habits to create personalized playlists like Discover Weekly.

9. **Google Assistant**  
Uses speech recognition and NLP to understand voice commands and respond accurately.

10. **Paytm Fraud Detection**  
ML detects unusual transaction patterns to prevent fraud and improve security.
