## Project Title
Student Performance Predictor

---

## Problem
Students sometimes struggle academically.
Teachers need a way to identify at-risk students early.

Goal:
Predict student performance based on study behaviour.

---

## Dataset
Example features:

Study hours  
Attendance percentage  
Assignment scores  
Previous exam marks  

Label:
Final performance category (High / Medium / Low)

Dataset can be created manually or collected from survey.

---

## Algorithm
Linear Regression or Decision Tree

Reason:
Easy to interpret
Works well for small datasets

---

## Expected Output

Prediction of student performance level.

Example:

Input:
Study hours = 5
Attendance = 80%

Output:
Performance = Medium

---

## Impact
Helps teachers provide support early.
Improves academic results.
Encourages better study habits.
