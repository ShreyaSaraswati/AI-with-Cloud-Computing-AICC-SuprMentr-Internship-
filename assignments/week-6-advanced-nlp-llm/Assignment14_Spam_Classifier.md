# Assignment 14 — Spam Classifier Thinking

## Objective
Design spam detection system conceptually.

---

## Problem

Email systems must automatically identify spam messages.

Goal:
classify message as spam or not spam.

---

## Possible Features

word frequency
presence of promotional keywords
message length
special characters
capital letters

Examples:

free
win
offer
discount

---

## Dataset Required

labeled messages:

spam
not spam

example:

"Win free iPhone"
spam

"Meeting at 5pm"
not spam

---

## Possible Errors

false positive:
normal email classified as spam

false negative:
spam not detected

---

## Expected Output

message category:
spam
not spam

---

## Key Learnings

feature engineering impacts performance  
classification problems require labeled data  
evaluation metrics important
